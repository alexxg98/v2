A dead zone, or hypoxia, is an area in the ocean where oxygen is depleted. This results in an enviromental uninhabitable by marine life.
  1. Environmentally, dead zones result in a loss of biodiversity and data depletes our fresh water sources
  2. Economically, dead zones harm the marine and the surrounding ecosystems, which in turn damages the farming industry
  3. Dead zones affect the water and our food source, making them harmful to consume
